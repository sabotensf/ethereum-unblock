The Problem

Music Rights Are Broken

  • ▲ Opaque ownership. Physical vinyl records carry zero verifiable provenance — no chain of custody, no onchain identity.
  • ▲ Fragmented rights. Music rights are split across labels, distributors, and publishers with no single source of truth.
  • ▲ Slow, centralized royalties. Artists wait 12–18 months for royalty payments routed through opaque intermediaries.
  • ▲ No phygital bridge. The physical record in your hands is disconnected from any digital rights, streaming data, or onchain value.
  • ▲ Counterfeit risk. Bootleg pressings are undetectable — there's no hardware-level authentication for physical media.
The Solution

One Tap. Provenance Proven. Royalties Paid.

💿

Hardware Authentication

Every record is embedded with an NTAG 424 DNA SDM NFC chip. The chip generates a cryptographically unique, tamper-evident URL on every tap — hardware-enforced authenticity.

🔗

Onchain Attestation

Each registered record is attested via EAS (Ethereum Attestation Service) on Sepolia. The UID is the immutable anchor linking physical chip to onchain identity.

🎵

Gated Audio Playback

Tapping unlocks the artist's master recording — a direct connection between owning the physical record and accessing the digital work.

⚡

Instant Royalty Splits

0xSplits distributes royalties on-demand: 80% artist / 20% RecordPool. No intermediaries. No 18-month wait. Programmable, transparent, and auditable.

Tap Flow

How It Works

01
📱

iPhone Tap

User holds phone to NFC chip on vinyl record. Chip generates unique URL with UID + hardware play counter.

›
02
🔐

Server Session

API validates the SDM signature, creates a tap session, and redirects browser to the /tap page.

›
03
☁

EAS Query

Client queries Ethereum Attestation Service on Sepolia — looks up the UID to verify onchain record provenance.

›
04
🎵

Audio Unlock

On attestation match: audio playback unlocks and a 0xSplits royalty distribution is triggered automatically.

›
05
⚡

Splits Pay Out

80% flows to the artist. 20% to RecordPool. Onchain, immediate, auditable by anyone.
